Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster

Description

Considered the "Best Drink in Existence" the Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster was invented by ex-Galactic President Zaphod Beeblebrox. Drinking one is likened to having your brains smashed out by a slice of lemon wrapped round a large gold brick.

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle Ol' Janx Spirit
  • 1 measure water from the seas of Santraginus V
  • 3 cubes Arcturan Mega-gin (frozen)
  • 4 litres Fallian marsh gas
  • 1 measure Qualactin Hypermint extract
  • 1 tooth of Algolian Suntiger
  • Sprinkle of Zamphuor
  • Olive for garnish

Directions

  1. Take juice from bottle of Ol' Janx Spirit and pout into it the sea water from Santraginus V.
  2. Allow the cubes of Arcturan Mega-Gin to melt into the mixture
  3. Bubble all 4 litres of Fallian marsh gas throught the mxture
  4. Over the back of a silver spoon float the Qualactin Hypermint extract
  5. Drop in Suntiger tooth; watch it dissolve
  6. Add a sprinkling of Zamphuor
  7. Garnish with olive
  8. Drink up! (very carefullly)
