Squid

Description

A culinary disaster that will have you feeling like you've been violated by a giant squid.

Ingredients

  • Dried Squid Tentacles
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey (optional)

Directions

  1. Grill squid tentacles until desired texture
  2. While squid is grilling, heat peanut butter over medium heat. Add honey if desired.
  3. Toss squid in peanut butter sauce
  4. Serve
